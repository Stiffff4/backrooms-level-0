BACKROOMS WIKI ADAPTATION
-------------------------

This game adapts “Level 0 — Threshold” from Backrooms Wiki:
https://backrooms-wiki.wikidot.com/level-0

Credited authors: DivineAtlas, DrAkimoto and RobertGoerman, plus contributors identified in the
revision history of the source page.
Source license: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported (CC BY-SA 3.0).
License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/
Wiki licensing guide: https://backrooms-wiki.wikidot.com/licensing-guide

Adapted elements include the Level 0 name and general description, its yellow office architecture,
fluorescent hum, damp carpet, and the concept of a flickering wall as an exit toward Level 1.
THRESHOLD changes these elements into a seeded procedural first-person game with original runtime
geometry, procedural textures, synthesized audio, controlled anomalies and an original ending UI.
No images from the wiki were copied. Backrooms Wiki and its contributors do not endorse this game.
